Tagline
The Gateway to Global Success

Vision
To become a gateway to global success through personalized, interactive, and internationally recognized English learning—empowering every student to achieve their academic, professional, and personal goals.

Mission

  • To provide an enjoyable and communicative English learning experience with materials relevant to each learner’s needs.
  • To offer personalized guidance for specific goals — from General and Business English to international test preparation (IELTS, TOEFL, PTE).
  • To apply international standards (CEFR, Cambridge, British Council, Pearson, IDP) to ensure high-quality teaching.
  • To utilize digital technology (Zoom/Google Meet with lifetime class recordings) for flexible learning.
  • To deliver direct feedback and effective study strategies, helping students progress quickly and confidently.
  • To continuously enhance teaching quality through international certifications, professional experience, and ongoing training.

About Us
English with Arik was founded by I Putu Arik Budiarsana, a professional English tutor with over 13 years of teaching experience, including more than four years online. We are dedicated to helping learners of all ages and backgrounds—both in Indonesia and abroad—reach their English goals.

Why Choose English with Arik?

  • Internationally certified tutor (TESOL/TEFL 120 hours, TEOL, IELTS Speaking & Writing by IDP, PTE by Pearson, and certified educator by the Ministry of Education).
  • Flexible programs: General English (CEFR A1–C2), Business English, English for Specific Purposes, and IELTS/TOEFL/PTE preparation.
  • Trusted, high-quality materials from Cambridge, the British Council, Pearson, Collins, and IDP.
  • Communicative and interactive teaching approach, with lifetime access to class recordings for review.
  • Personalized feedback (real-time and delayed) and test simulations for optimal preparation.
  • Trusted by local and Indonesian diaspora students worldwide.
